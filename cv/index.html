Projects

  • 2024.02 - present
    Comorbid AUD/Panic Disorder Model
    Investigating neurobiology underlying comorbid psychiatric disorders at behavioral, molecular, and circuit level. Characterized a novel rodent model using homeostatic-stressor CO2-inhalation as panicogenic challenge.
    • Found that withdrawal from chronic ethanol increased sensitivity to CO2-inhalation in C57Bl/6J but not DBA/2J
    • Developed custom FIJI script enabling user-friendly, Ilastik powered quantification for cFos positive cells
    • Manuscript in preparation
    • Presented at UIC Dept. of Psychiatry, SfN 2024, RSA 2025
  • 2024.02 - present
    TDAG8 in Alcohol Use Disorder
    Investigating role of microglial acid sensor TDAG8 in regulating AUD-associated outcomes and drinking behaviors.
    • Studied voluntary consumption in DID model, EtOH metabolism, ataxia, locomotor stimulation, and respiratory changes
    • Found TDAG8 inhibitor decreased EtOH consumption and microglial activation
    • Presented at UIC Undergraduate Research Forum
  • 2024.02 - present
    Brain-wide Functional Network Analysis
    Investigating brain-wide functional network disruptions in model of comorbid AUD/PD using tissue-clearing techniques to quantify cFos expression.
    • Developing network analysis to identify hub regions modulating differential sensitivity to CO2
    • Honors thesis project
    • Will result in publication
  • 2025.05 - 2025.08
    Statistical Mechanics and Neural Systems
    Reading project exploring Ising models, message passing, Metropolis Monte Carlo simulations, replica method, replica symmetry breaking, and dimensionality reduction.
    • Developed toolkit in Python enabling simulation and analysis of Ising systems
    • Presented talk: 'From Monte Carlo Simulations to Analytic Solutions of Ising Models'
  • 2025.01 - 2025.05
    Graph Representation Learning
    Reading project on graph convolutional networks and their relation to image convolution and spectral theory; graph theoretic measures relevant to neural networks.
    • Presented talk: 'Graph Convolutional Networks (as an extension of convolution over grids)'
